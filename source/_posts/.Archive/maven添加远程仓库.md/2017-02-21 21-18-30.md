# Java.net资源库
> 添加Java.net远程仓库的详细信息在“pom.xml”文件
``` xml
<!--pom.xml-->
<project ...>
<repositories>
    <repository>
      <id>java.net</id>
      <url>https://maven.java.net/content/repositories/public/</url>
    </repository>
 </repositories>
</project>
```
